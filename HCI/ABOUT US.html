OUR MISSION

One of the greatest ways to express feelings is to say it with flowers. It has been many years where the universal reason we give flowers is to communicate and further our connection with one another. It is truly a gift of meaning.



KRN's FLOWER SHOP

KRN's FLOWER SHOP is the largest and most trusted online flower delivery company in South East Asia with fulfillment centers in The Philippines, Malaysia, Singapore, Hong Kong and Indonesia.



Although we are based in Makati, Manila, our orders are placed from all over the globe; to have flower gifts delivered to their loved ones in the Philippines. No matter where you are, Flower Chimp is determine to deliver happiness. One blossom at a time. Our integrity is supported by the “Flower Chimp Guarantee” ensuring unbeatable freshness, superior quality and the best value for every single order.
